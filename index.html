Frequently Asked Questions

AI chatbots are computer programs powered by large language models (LLMs) that can understand and respond to human input in natural language. They can assist with various tasks including writing, analysis, coding, and general conversation.

Consider factors like your specific needs (coding, writing, analysis), pricing model (free vs paid), integration requirements, and specialized capabilities. Some models excel at technical tasks while others are better for creative work or conversation.

Many AI chatbots offer free tiers with basic functionality, while advanced features are often available through paid subscriptions. Some models like ChatGPT and Claude have both free and premium versions with different capabilities and usage limits.

Privacy and safety vary by provider. Most major AI chatbots have strict privacy policies and data handling practices. However, it's important to review each service's terms and avoid sharing sensitive personal information in conversations with AI.
